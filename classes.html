Our Training Platform

Discover a variety of aerial and circus training programs designed for all skill levels.

From beginners to advanced performers, explore classes that build strength, flexibility, and confidence.

Read More

🎪Aerial Silks

Graceful movements performed using suspended fabric in the air. Build strength, flexibility, and body control through guided routines. Perfect for those who love artistic expression and aerial flow.

🎪Trapeze

Experience the thrill of swinging and balancing on a trapeze bar. Learn techniques that improve coordination, strength, and confidence. Suitable for beginners and advanced students alike.

🎪Acrobatics

Enhance flexibility, balance, and core strength through floor training. Learn flips, rolls, and dynamic movements in a safe environment. Ideal for building a strong foundation.

🎪Beginner Classes

Start your aerial journey with step-by-step guided training. Focus on basic techniques, safety, and body conditioning. No prior experience needed – open for all age groups.

🎪Advanced Training

Take your skills to the next level with intensive training sessions. Master complex tricks, transitions, and performance techniques. Designed for experienced students.
