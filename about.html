About The Aerial School

AeroCircus, We have been teaching aerial arts since 2001

Our studio specializes in aerial silks, trapeze, and acrobatics training. Students develop strength, flexibility, and coordination through structured programs designed for all levels. Our experienced instructors focus on safety, technique, and performance skills, creating a supportive environment for learning. We aim to inspire confidence, creativity, and passion in every student while helping them grow and achieve their personal goals.

At Aero Circus School, we specialize in aerial silks, circus arts, and creative movement training. Our mission is to help students build strength, flexibility, and confidence while exploring the beauty of aerial performance and artistic expression.
